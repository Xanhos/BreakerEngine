enum ParticlesTypes

Enumerates the different types of particle behaviors.

Enumerator
NONE 

No particle behavior.

ONE_TIME 

Particles that exist once and do not loop.

LIFE_TIME 

Particles that have a lifetime and despawn after a set time.

ALWAYS 

Particles that continue indefinitely.
